SUMMARY

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. Whether you are looking for a mechanical engineer, a materials scientist, or someone passionate about semiconductor technologies, I’m glad you’re here. I am originally from Bangladesh, and my journey in engineering has taken me across disciplines and geographies. And yes—while I’d enjoy a good debate on whether the earth is round or flat (just kidding), I’m especially excited to connect with people interested in engineering, innovation, and problem solving.

I enjoy working across disciplines and tackling complex, real-world challenges—from materials processing and device fabrication to data-driven analysis. I like to think of myself not as a specialist in a single box, but as an engineer driven by curiosity and the desire to build, optimize, and create impactful solutions.

If you’re interested in collaboration, challenging ideas, or meaningful engineering work, I hope this portfolio gives you a clear sense of what I bring to the table.

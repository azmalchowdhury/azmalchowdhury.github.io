About Me

PhD in Materials Science & Engineering with expertise in semiconductor device fabrication, thin film deposition, and micro/nanofabrication. Experienced in cleanroom processes including PVD, ALD, RIE, and photolithography, with strong focus on process optimization, DOE, and device prototyping.

Fabrication and exploration of HfSe₂-based FET devices using electrochemical exfoliation and thin film integration.

IDE Fabrication on PDMS

Process Data Analysis (DOE/SPC)

Applied JMP, Python, and statistical methods for process optimization, variability reduction, and yield improvement.

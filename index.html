About Me

Semiconductor Process Engineer | Device Fabrication | Data-Driven Optimization

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Materials Science and Engineering with a strong foundation in semiconductor process engineering, thin-film fabrication, and micro/nanofabrication. My work bridges high-volume manufacturing experience with advanced device prototyping, enabling me to approach engineering problems with both practical and research-driven perspectives.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I gained over three years of industrial experience in high-volume manufacturing environments, including steel production and gas transmission systems. As a Mechanical Maintenance Engineer and later as a shift in-charge, I led equipment troubleshooting, preventive maintenance, and continuous operation of critical infrastructure, developing a strong foundation in reliability, root-cause analysis, and process stability.

During my Ph.D., I focused on semiconductor device fabrication and process development, working extensively in cleanroom environments. My experience includes thin-film deposition (PVD, CVD), photolithography, etching, and process integration. I have developed and optimized fabrication workflows, established SOPs and run cards, and applied data-driven methodologies such as Design of Experiments (DOE) and Statistical Process Control (SPC) to improve process performance and reproducibility.

I also have extensive experience in material and device characterization, including SEM, AFM, Raman spectroscopy, FTIR, and nanoindentation, enabling in-depth failure analysis and performance evaluation. In addition, I have worked on sensor integration and electronics development, including projects related to wearable and cuffless physiological monitoring systems.

I am an inventor with multiple granted patents and ongoing disclosures. My combined experience in manufacturing and semiconductor process engineering allows me to effectively translate research into scalable engineering solutions. I am particularly interested in roles focused on process development, integration, and optimization in advanced semiconductor manufacturing.

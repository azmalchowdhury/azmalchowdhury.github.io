Paper-Based Supercapacitive Pressure Sensor

ACS AMI First Author Wearable Pulse Waveform

First-ever tissue-paper-based supercapacitive pressure sensor using commercially available conductive textiles. Achieved 2.9 kPa⁻¹ sensitivity (0-16 kPa), 1.5 kPa⁻¹ (16-90 kPa), 0.2s response time, and 5000-cycle stability for wrist arterial pulse waveform monitoring.
